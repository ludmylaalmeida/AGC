Our Story

How Everything Started

Almeida General Construction combines over 15 years of woodworking experience to build quality cabinets, furniture and other custom wood elements for your home or business. We carefully build custom cabinets, staircases, decks, bars, and other fine wood elements. We strive to meet and exceed our customers' expectations at all times.

With thousands of projects completed in New England, you can be assured we have references for every type of project.

Our Services

What We Offer

Custom Woodwork

A design team is available to help you find the perfect design for your project. Cabinets 100% custom-designed to fit your needs and budget with professional installation by our expert team.

Residential Construction

From initial conception followed by design to final build. We have experience getting the necessary permits and making the process easy for the home owner.

Commercial Construction

As a General Contractor we are committed to all levels of construction including commercial construction. From small projects to large scale ones we can always handle our clients' needs.
